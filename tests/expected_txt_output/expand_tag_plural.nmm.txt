§ 2    Our main results
       ────────────────

¶ 2.1  THEOREMS (Main)

       (a)   Nothing lasts forever.

       (b)   All is well iff

             (1)   nothing is bad, and

             (2)   I know that '[ITM:2+]' is rendered nicely, like this:
                   (+).

             (+)   I know that '[ITM:2a]' is rendered nicely, like this:
                   (1).

       (X)   '[ITM:2a]' -> (b)(1)

       ─     '[ITM:2+]' -> (b)(+)

       '[ITM:2a]' -> (b)(1)

       '[ITM:2+]' -> (b)(+)

       '[ITM:1]' -> (a)

       '[ITM:X]' -> (X)


¶ 2.2  PROOF (of Theorems 2.1)  By straightforward induction on the
       complexity of nothing.
