§ 1

¶ 1.1  THEOREM (Main)

       (a)   Nothing lasts forever.

       (b)   All is well.

             (1)   sometimes

             (2)   forever

       '[ITM:1]' -> (a)

       '[ITM:2]' -> (b)

       '[ITM:2a]' -> (b)(1)

       '[ITM:2b]' -> (b)(2)


¶ 1.2  PROOF (of Theorem 1.1)  By straightforward induction on the
       complexity of nothing.

       '[THM:main]' -> Theorem 1.1

       '[ITM:1]' -> (a) of Theorem 1.1

       '[ITM:2]' -> (b) of Theorem 1.1

       '[ITM:2a]' -> (b)(1) of Theorem 1.1

       '[ITM:2b]' -> (b)(2) of Theorem 1.1
